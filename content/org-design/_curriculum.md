# Org Design & The Future of Companies

> *How companies think, coordinate, and compound — and how AI is changing all three.*

This track teaches the first principles of organisational design: why hierarchy exists, why it persists, and what it means that AI can now replace the function it serves. It draws from strategy, management history, and live company experiments.

## Why this track exists

Kevin is building toward being an AI-native CPO. Understanding org design at the first-principles level — not the MBA-buzzword level — is a prerequisite for that role. Every CPO conversation for the next decade will involve opinions about how AI changes the coordination problem. This track builds those opinions from the ground up.
