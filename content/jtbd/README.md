# JTBD — Jobs to Be Done
## A Bobby Moesta Teaching Series

> *"People don't buy products; they hire them to do a Job in their life and make progress."*
> — Bobby Moesta

This notebook is built entirely from Bobby Moesta's live sessions at Memrise (January–March 2026), his Growth Summit slides, and 30 years of case studies from The Re-Wired Group.

Bobby teaches at Northwestern's Kellogg School and MIT. He co-created Jobs to Be Done with Clay Christensen and has applied it across 3,500+ products.
